# Ground

The values in `core.md` are essential and abstract. This file is the ground they stand on — a reading of what each value already looks like alive in InsideOut's own material. Not a style guide, not a rulebook. A place for a writer, a program, or a sentence to stand when the values need to become words.

---

## The two registers are one inside

InsideOut speaks in two voices. The formal documents — the verksamhetsberättelse, the 2026 verksamhetsplan, the stadgar — speak in Swedish institutional language: *psykosocial stabilitet, tvärprofessionella metoder, handlingskraft, egenmakt.* The public LinkedIn posts speak in English, in the register of interior awakening: *creators of reality, the holographic principle, presence, surrender, the dreamer of the dream.*

A reader who meets both can feel like they are meeting two organizations. They are not. They are two rooms, and the rooms ask for different voices. A grant document in the awakening register would lose its audience. A LinkedIn post in the institutional register would reach no one. The voices are answers to the rooms.

What holds them together is the inside. Both registers say *healing begins inside* — one as *läkning börjar inifrån*, the other as *you are the one dreaming the dream*. Both say *a person is a creator* — one as *egenmakt och etableringsförmåga*, the other as *every human being is an ultimate creator*. The surface changes with the room. The inside does not.

A sentence belongs to InsideOut when it answers to the five values, in whatever register the room asks for.

## What each value looks like alive

### Healing begins inside

In the lineage: the founders did not set out to build a therapy organization. They set out to meet displaced families in Help Ukraine Gothenburg, and discovered in the daily work that what needed meeting was not only the war, but what the war had left in each person's interior. The turn toward inside-out was the only honest response to what was in the rooms.

In the posts: *"Healing from trauma is an inside-out process. It starts within — by meeting your inner world with honesty and compassion — and then unfolds into how you show up in life, relationships, and the world."*

### A person is already whole

Nova Klubb is not remediation. The 320 children in it are not being fixed. What they are offered — swimming, museum days, a Lucia concert, a photo contest called *Pass It Forward*, Christmas gifts — is a context in which the wholeness each child is already carrying has room to come forward. Nothing is being installed.

In the posts: *"Your true self has been there all along. Waiting."*

### A person is a creator

The stadgar list *personlig utveckling och empowerment* and *mindful och hållbart ledarskap* as purposes — the institutional names for the same claim the posts make as *"we are the ultimate creators of reality"* and *"from passenger to creator."* Same inside, two rooms.

### Pain is a doorway

*Healing Beyond Fear* — the method book a physician, a psychologist, and an author are writing together — is not a pain management program. It is a program about meeting fear, stress, and old experience as doorways to what is underneath them. The framing is not *reduce the suffering* but *honor it, and watch what it opens.*

In the posts: *"The pain is real. The grief, the fear, the disorientation — none of it should be dismissed or bypassed. We must honor it all. And yet, when life cracks us open in these tender places, something profound can happen."*

### Inside and outside are one motion

The three domains — **Next Generation**, **Personal Evolution**, **Connected Society** — are one motion running through three scales of human life. The child's inside shapes the adult they become. The adult's inside shapes what they bring to others. What people bring to each other becomes the society they share. The motion runs from inside outward, and any piece of work that touches only one scale is touching only one part of one motion.

In the posts: *"Reality is not something that happens to us. It is something that emerges from us."* *"If conflict originates in the mind, then so does peace."*

Where it grows is into a concrete communication framework — markers for what each register sounds like when the values are in it, what it sounds like when the values have drifted, and how a writer can test a sentence before it goes out.
